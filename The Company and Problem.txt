The company - Meadow Vet Care, a modern Irish veterinary clinic serving dogs, cats, rabbits, small mammals, and birds. They offer 94 services across 13 categories including consultations, preventive care, vaccinations, surgery, dentistry, diagnostics, grooming, behaviour, and more. Their differentiator is compassionate, personal care for every animal and owner, delivered from their clinic at 14 Greenfield Road, Dublin 6.

The problem - Customers call the clinic to ask the same repetitive questions about services, prices, offers, and availability. These queries tie up reception staff and frustrate customers who just want quick answers. How can Meadow Vet Care provide instant, accurate service information online, 24/7, without losing the personal touch that defines them? A friendly AI chatbot connected directly to the live service catalogue could come to the rescue.
