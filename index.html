💾 What MeadowBot stores

MeadowBot remembers a few things to make your visits better -- all stored on your device, never sent to a server:

  • Your name (if you tell her)
  • Services and categories you've looked up
  • How many times you've visited
  • Your last visit date
  • Your pet's name (if you book)

This lets MeadowBot greet you by name, recall past queries, and pick up where you left off. To remove everything, click Clear memory beside MeadowBot's status badge.
